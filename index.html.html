The complete picture

Listen first. Then look outward.

The most important intelligence starts with your own customers. Before you look at rivals, understand deeply what the people already choosing you are telling you. Then use competitor insight as your second weapon.

Your customer voice

Hear what your customers are really saying

Every review is a direct signal from a real customer. Review Snipper aggregates and analyses all of them — so you get the clear, honest picture of what your business is doing well and what it needs to fix.

Brand health dashboard
Your overall rating trend, sentiment breakdown, and review volume in one clear view. Know at a glance whether your brand is growing stronger or drifting.
Theme & topic analysis
What do customers keep mentioning? Staff, quality, wait times, value? Ranked by frequency across all platforms, so you know exactly what matters most to the people paying you.
Instant negative review alert
The moment a 1-star or 2-star review lands on any platform for your own business, you get an immediate email with the review text and platform. Respond fast, recover the relationship.
Alerts apply to your own brand reviews only — this is about protecting your own reputation, not monitoring rivals.
Recurring friction detection
Negative themes appearing across multiple reviews are flagged and prioritised. Fix issues while they are still recoverable — before they show up in your rating.
Competitor insight

Then see what rivals’ customers are saying

Once you know your own position clearly, look outward. Run a competitor analysis whenever you choose — before a campaign, quarterly review, or whenever you want a market pulse.

Competitor analysis on demand
Run a competitor analysis on your own schedule. Review Snipper fetches and clusters their reviews by theme, so you see exactly where rivals are failing their customers — on your terms.
Rating trajectory tracking
Is a competitor recovering or continuing to slide? Trend data tells you exactly when to press the advantage and when to hold your position and watch.
AI Action Playbook
The AI combines both views — your brand gaps and competitor weaknesses — into a specific, ranked action plan. Not generic advice. Built from your actual data against theirs.
Local competitor radius search
Reviews are location-specific. Search for competitors by name or use your current location coordinates to find nearby rivals. Analyse only businesses you genuinely compete with — not irrelevant results from other cities or regions.
Why it matters

Your customers are already telling you the answers

The information to fix your business, grow your reputation, and win more customers is already there — written in the reviews your customers leave every day. Most businesses never fully hear it.

Scattered across five platforms, buried in volume, with no clear picture of what matters most — the voice of your customers is valuable intelligence that most businesses simply cannot access. Review Snipper changes that.

Your customer voice is scattered and unclear
Reviews are spread across Google, Trustpilot, Yelp and more. You see a star rating but miss the pattern — the recurring praise to amplify, the friction points quietly costing you reviews every week.
Negative reviews damage you before you respond
A 1-star review can sit live for days before you notice it. By then it has already influenced potential customers. Instant alerts let you respond in minutes — often turning a complaint into a resolved 4-star.
Competitor intelligence is an untapped advantage
Your rivals’ customers are also leaving public reviews exposing exactly where those businesses are failing. Most owners never read them. Every one is an opportunity to step into a gap your competitors are leaving open.
